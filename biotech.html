Course Description

This course introduces the fundamental concepts of biotechnology, including molecular biology, genetic engineering, bioprocess technology, and applications in medicine, agriculture, and industry. Students will gain an understanding of basic techniques and principles used in biotechnology research and development.

Course Objectives

  • Understand core concepts of biotechnology
  • Learn molecular biology and genetic engineering techniques
  • Study bioprocess technology and industrial applications
  • Understand the role of biotechnology in medicine and agriculture
  • Discuss recent advances and future trends

Course Description

This course introduces the fundamental concepts of agricultural machinery used in crop production and post-harvest operations. It covers farm power sources, tillage machinery, sowing and planting equipment, harvesting machines, and machinery for processing agricultural produce. Students will learn about machine design, working principles, performance evaluation and recent technological developments in agricultural mechanization.

Course Objectives

  • Understand farm power and agricultural machinery systems
  • Learn working principles of field and processing equipment
  • Study design and operation of farm machinery
  • Understand farm mechanization and technology adoption
  • Discuss machinery safety and future developments
